# Privacy Policy

This application ("the App") respects user privacy and is committed to protecting personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled within the App.

1. Information Collected

The App may access the following information:

Notification information provided by the Android operating system
(such as app name, notification title, and notification channel ID)

Notification rules and voice messages configured by the user within the App

Minimal technical information required for the App to function properly

The App does not collect personally identifiable information such as name, address, phone number, or email address.

2. Purpose of Use

The collected information is used solely for the following purposes:

To provide voice announcements for notifications based on user-defined conditions

To operate and improve the App’s functionality

3. Data Storage and Management

All information handled by the App is stored only on the user’s device.
No data is transmitted to the developer or any external servers.

4. Data Sharing

The App does not share collected information with third parties, except when required by law or with explicit user consent.

5. Permissions

The App uses notification access permission solely to detect notifications and provide voice announcements.
This permission is not used for any other purpose.

6. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as necessary. Any changes will become effective upon publication within the App or on the distribution page.
