# 🔰 Getting Started

Smart Notifire (referred to as "this app" below) announces received notifications by voice.  
Therefore, immediately after installation, you cannot add voice announcement rules until the app receives a notification.  
For checking how the app works, this app includes one voice announcement rule in advance.

<img alt="Basic Rule" src="https://teyandei.github.io/SmartNotifire-Rev2/images/basic_rule.png" title="Basic Rule" style="width:clamp(30%,calc(60000px - 10000vw),80vw);height:auto;max-width:100%;"/>
  
Let's try this voice announcement rule and confirm how the app works.  

> 🔆 You can check the basic behavior with only tap operations.

##  1️⃣ Launch the app

When you launch the app for the first time, the settings screen for "Notification access" is displayed.  
Enable it by following the on-screen instructions.  
  
Note: This permission is used only to read notification titles aloud.  
  
##  2️⃣ Check notifications

<img alt="Check Notification" src="https://teyandei.github.io/SmartNotifire-Rev2/images/notification_check.png" title="Notification Check Screen" style="width:clamp(30%,calc(60000px - 10000vw),80vw);height:auto;max-width:100%;"/>

1. Tap the **Notify** button to send a test notification from this app.
2. A dialog asking whether to allow notifications is displayed. Tap **Allow**.    
   <img alt="Allow Notification" src="https://teyandei.github.io/SmartNotifire-Rev2/images/allow_notification.png" title="Notification Permission" style="width:clamp(30%,calc(60000px - 10000vw),80vw);height:auto;max-width:100%;"/>
3. The notification is sent, and the notification sound plays.  

##  3️⃣ Enable the voice announcement rule
    
<img alt="Enable Rule" src="https://teyandei.github.io/SmartNotifire-Rev2/images/enable_rule.png" title="Enable Rule" style="width:clamp(30%,calc(60000px - 10000vw),80vw);height:auto;max-width:100%;"/>
  
- Tap the permission switch to enable the voice announcement rule.  

##  4️⃣ Check the voice announcement

- Tap the **Notify** button from step 2️⃣ again.
- The notification sound plays, and after 3 seconds, the voice message is read aloud.

> This is the basic behavior of this app. Next, see [Adding a voice announcement rule](./add_rule).
